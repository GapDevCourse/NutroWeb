Diabetes-Friendly Recipes

Low-Carb Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

Grill the chicken breast and slice it. Toss it with mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumber, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.

Health Tip: This recipe is low in carbs and packed with protein, making it perfect for managing blood sugar levels.

Heart-Healthy Recipes

Oatmeal with Berries and Almonds

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond milk (unsweetened)
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries)
  • 2 tbsp sliced almonds

Instructions:

Cook oats with almond milk. Top with mixed berries and sliced almonds.

Health Tip: This oatmeal is high in fiber and antioxidants, which are great for heart health.

Gluten-Free Recipes

Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric

Instructions:

Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Roast vegetables with olive oil and turmeric. Combine cooked quinoa and roasted vegetables.

Health Tip: Quinoa is a great gluten-free grain that is rich in protein and fiber, making it a perfect base for a healthy meal.
