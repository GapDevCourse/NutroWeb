Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350°F for 25 minutes.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pasta
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peppers, broccoli, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Cook pasta, sauté vegetables with garlic and olive oil, and combine with pasta. Season with salt and pepper.

Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups apples (sliced)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 package pie crust

Instructions:

Place apples in the pie crust, mix sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg, and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
